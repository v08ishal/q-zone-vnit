// CREDIT FORMULA (L-T-P)

1 tutorial hr/week = 1 credit
1 practical hr/week = 0.5 credit

// ABOUT ECE DEPT — VNIT NAGPUR
Established in 1984, the ECE Department at VNIT Nagpur has grown to a B.Tech intake of 116. Both UG and PG programs are NBA-accredited for six years. Research areas include 5G/6G, RF & Antennas, Signal Processing, AI/ML, VLSI, IoT, and Biomedical Engineering. The department has produced GATE/CAT toppers and Institute Visvesvaraya Medalists. Alumni are highly placed with ~5% being entrepreneurs.
